# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Pharos to be a respectful, thoughtful, and high-signal project.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- being respectful in disagreement
- giving constructive feedback
- assuming good intent while staying precise
- welcoming contributors with different levels of experience
- focusing on the work, not the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- trolling or intentionally inflammatory behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- sustained disruptive behavior in issues, reviews, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior through the contact path in `SECURITY.md` until a dedicated community contact address is published.
